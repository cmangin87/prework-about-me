Dream Theater Fan Site!

Dream Theater is a progressive metal band formed in 1985 by guitarist John Petrucci, bassist John Myung, and drummer Mike Portnoy. Since then, they have gone through many different lineups andchanges in sound but remain my favorite band since I was 15 years old!

Reasons I like Dream Theater

  • They are expert musicians as most have studied music at prestigious music colleges like Berklee College of Music in Boston and the Julliard School of Music in New York City.
  • They have been making music and touring consistently for over 30 years.
  • They are able to reinvent themselves with each unique album showing their versatility as musicians.
